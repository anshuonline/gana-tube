Prohibited Ad Categories

GanaTube strictly prohibits the advertisement of the following categories to maintain a safe and family-friendly environment.

Adult Content

Any sexually explicit content, pornography, or suggestive imagery is strictly banned.

Gambling & Betting

Online casinos, sports betting, lotteries, and related gambling applications.

Illegal Substances

Drugs, tobacco products, vape products, and related paraphernalia.

Scams & Frauds

Get-rich-quick schemes, crypto scams, phishing links, and deceptive practices.

Hate Speech & Violence

Ads promoting discrimination, hate speech, bullying, or depicting graphic violence.

Misleading Claims

Fake news, false medical claims, or deceptive advertising that tricks users.

If your campaign falls into any of these categories, it will be rejected during the review process without refund (if paid).
